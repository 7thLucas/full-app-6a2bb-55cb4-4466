# InterviewIQ

## What It Does
AI-powered interview video reviewer. Users upload interview session videos and receive structured AI feedback on answer quality, communication clarity, confidence, and delivery.

## Users
- Primary: Job seekers preparing for interviews, wanting objective performance feedback
- Secondary: HR teams and hiring managers reviewing candidate interview sessions

## Core Problem
No objective, structured way to review interview performance. Candidates guess at what went wrong; hiring teams make subjective calls with no consistent rubric.

## Brand & Tone
Professional, confident, sharp. Like a smart senior recruiter giving honest, actionable feedback. Not harsh, not fluffy.

## MVP Scope
1. Upload interview video session
2. AI analyzes and scores: answer quality, communication clarity, body language cues, confidence level
3. Dashboard showing scores per question/segment with detailed feedback
4. Admin panel for managing sessions and users
